Down-Beat to Up-Beat can be used to:

  • Find other musicians in your area to connect with

  • Share ways to improve your music skills

  • Get some potential listeners by adding links to your SoundCloud and/or Spotify account

  • Choose whether you are joining just for some fun jam sessions, to make a band or maybe both

  • Boost your overall happiness and improve your mental health by using the site to meet up with other people with similar music interests in real life

Down-Beat to Up-Beat is a social site created to help connect musicians of similar levels, styles, interests and needs with each other
